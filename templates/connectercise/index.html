About Connectercise

Here at Connectercise we are dedicated to bringing you a new and unimagined experience, that involved a whole lot of burning calories and a whole lot more of making friends! Connectercise features exclusively:

  • A web application which allows users to meet each other and do sporting activities together
  • Users can create sporting requests which are categorized by sport and location
  • Users can fulfill the sporting requests of other users
  • Users can view the past sporting activity of other users, utilizing the Strava API
